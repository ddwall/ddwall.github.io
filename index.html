The International Difficulty Wall is a collection of the most difficult routines performed by atheltes all over the world in Trampoline, DMT, and Tumbling.

In order to be eligible, routines must:

Trampoline
  • Be done on normal 7x14 trampoline
  • The last skill must be landed on the bed (no mat)
  • Minimum DD 10.0
Double Mini Trampoline
  • Be done to proper height landing mats
  • Minimum DD 4.5
Tumbling
  • Be done to proper height landing mats
  • Minimum DD 3.5

Submit your most difficult routine by emailing trampolineddwall@gmail.com with your full name, gender, country, and a video of your routine.
